## 📋 Overview
Understanding how ticketing systems fit into SOC workflows — how alerts become tickets, how analysts track investigations, and why structured case management matters.

## 🎯 Key Concepts
- Why ticketing systems are essential in a SOC environment
- The alert → ticket → investigation → resolution workflow
- Popular SOC ticketing platforms: osTicket, ServiceNow, Jira, TheHive
- Ticket fields: severity, priority, assignee, SLA, resolution notes
- How SIEM alerts integrate with ticketing systems (manual vs automated)
- SLA (Service Level Agreement) targets by severity:

| Severity | Response SLA |
|----------|-------------|
| Critical | 15 minutes |
| High | 1 hour |
| Medium | 4 hours |
| Low | 24 hours |
